Brochure Website

Brochure websites are a simplified form of business websites. For businesses that know they need an online presence, but don’t want to invest a lot into it (maybe you’re confident you’ll continue to get most of your business from other sources), a simple brochure site that includes just a few pages that lay out the basics of what you do and provide contact information may be enough for you.

How to make a brochure online in 4 easy steps

Watch your brochure come to life in just a few clicks! Our online brochure maker helps you create beautiful, professional brochures with minimum effort and no coding or design skills. Make it unique by adding your own text and images and hit publish or download it for print.


  1. 1

    Choose a page format and a template

    The first step is to choose the page format which you’re going to use for the entire brochure. Now that’s out of the way you can decide between creating your brochure from scratch or using one of our free brochure templates, developed by our elite design team.

  2. 2

    Customize your brochure and make it unique

    Customizing is easy! Our free brochure maker is not just easy to use, but it’s also a powerful tool that allows you to really get creative. You can tweak and replace backgrounds, customize fonts, add your own text and images or use images from our own free, high-quality gallery.

  3. 3

    Publish online, download or print

    Once you’re happy with the brochure design, just hit the publish button to make your brochure available online. You’ll still be able to edit it at any time, just remember to hit the publish button when you’re done editing, to make your changes visible online. If you’d like to print your brochure you can easily do so, because we’ve made sure that your brochures look just as great printed as they look on screen.

  4. 4

    Share your brochure

    Don’t forget to share it with your friends, family, and customers. If you own a website you can also display your brochure there, this way your visitors will be able to access it directly on your website.
